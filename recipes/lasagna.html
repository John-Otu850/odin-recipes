Delicious Homemade Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort food made by layering wide pasta sheets with a rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and plenty of melted mozzarella. This hearty dish is baked until golden and bubbly, making it perfect for family dinners, gatherings, or a cozy night in.

In this recipe, we combine seasoned ground beef and tomato sauce with layers of pasta and cheese to create a mouthwatering, oven-baked masterpiece. Whether you're new to cooking or a kitchen pro, this lasagna is simple to follow and guaranteed to impress. Serve it with a side of garlic bread and a fresh salad for the ultimate homemade meal.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz (680g) marinara or pasta sauce
  • 15 oz (425g) ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps In Making The Recipe

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add diced onion and garlic; sauté until fragrant.
  4. Add the ground beef to the skillet. Cook until browned, then drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in the marinara or pasta sauce. Simmer for about 10 minutes, then season with salt and pepper.
  6. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the Parmesan cheese. Mix well.
  7. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom.
  8. Layer 3–4 noodles over the sauce, then spread a layer of the ricotta mixture, followed by meat sauce, and then a layer of mozzarella.
  9. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce and mozzarella on top. Sprinkle with remaining Parmesan cheese.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Then remove the foil and bake for another 15–20 minutes until the top is golden and bubbly.
  11. Let the lasagna rest for 10–15 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired.
